Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

MindShift is a real-time communication coach. To do its job it has to listen to your conversation and reason about the words — so it sends audio and text to two specialist third parties for processing. This page explains exactly what data that involves, where it goes, and how to have it erased. In plain language, and without dark patterns.

The short version. MindShift streams your microphone audio to Deepgram for live transcription, and sends the resulting conversation text to Anthropic (Claude) to generate coaching suggestions. Session transcripts are stored on MindShift's own server. We do not sell your data, and there are no ads. You can request deletion at any time — see the Data & Account Deletion page.

Who we are

MindShift ("we", "us") is an independent, pre-launch project. You can reach a human at sagearbor+mindshift@gmail.com.

What data we handle, and why

MindShift does not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, or contacts to use the app. The data we handle is the data the coaching feature needs to function:

Microphone audio

When you run a session, your microphone audio is streamed to Deepgram, a speech-to-text provider, which converts it to text in real time. MindShift does not save that audio as recordings — it exists only transiently to produce the transcript. Deepgram processes the audio under its own terms; see Deepgram's privacy policy.

Conversation text (transcripts)

The transcribed text of your conversation is the working material of the coach. It is sent to Anthropic's Claude model to generate suggestions, and it is stored on MindShift's own server (currently a small SQLite database) so a session holds together and you can look back at it. See Anthropic's privacy policy.

Who we share data with

We share data with two processors, only so the product can function:

  • Deepgram — real-time speech-to-text transcription of your microphone audio.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — generating the coaching suggestions from the conversation text.

These are the only third parties your conversation data is shared with, and only for the processing described above. We do not sell your data. We do not share it for advertising, and MindShift contains no ads.

How your data is protected

All data sent between the app, our server, and our processors travels over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to the stored transcripts is limited to what is needed to operate and debug the service.

How long we keep it, and how to delete it

Session transcripts remain on our server until you ask us to delete them (or until we retire the pilot). You can request erasure at any time, and you don't need an account to do so. Full instructions are on the Data & Account Deletion page, or email sagearbor+mindshift@gmail.com.

Children

MindShift is intended for adults (18+). It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we change how MindShift handles data, we'll update this page and its "last updated" date. Because the product is pre-launch, the way it works may evolve; we'll keep this page honest about what's actually happening.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email sagearbor+mindshift@gmail.com.

A note on what MindShift is. MindShift is a communication coaching tool, not a substitute for therapy, counseling, or professional medical or mental-health advice. It does not diagnose or treat any condition. If you or someone else is in crisis, please contact a qualified professional or your local emergency services.
