What it does

Support in the moment, not a lecture afterward.

Most advice arrives too late — after the words are already out. MindShift works while you're still in the conversation, so the kinder version is the one you actually say.

Real-time transcription

It follows the conversation live and keeps track of who said what — so its guidance is grounded in what's actually happening, not guesswork.

Gentle, spoken suggestions

When things get tense, a calm voice offers a warmer way to say it — quietly, in your ear, so only you hear the nudge.

Tuned to your tone

Turn the empathy dial from direct to deeply validating. MindShift adapts its suggestions to sound like the version of you that you want to be.

How it works

Three quiet steps, one better conversation.

No dashboards to manage mid-talk. You focus on the person in front of you; MindShift does the rest in the background.

01

Listens

Start a session and MindShift transcribes the conversation in real time, gently noting each speaker.

02

Understands

It reads the emotional undercurrent — where things are heating up, where someone feels unheard.

03

Suggests

At the right moment, it offers a kinder phrasing at the empathy level you chose — yours to use or ignore.

The empathy dial

You set the tone. Always.

The same feeling can be said a dozen ways. Slide from clear-and-direct to deeply-validating and watch a real example shift — this is the choice MindShift puts in your hands, live in every conversation.

Privacy by design

The most intimate conversations never have to leave the room.

For sensitive talks, MindShift can run transcription fully on-device or self-hosted — no audio sent to the cloud. Privacy isn't a setting you hunt for; it's the starting point.

On-device transcription. Choose a mode where audio is processed on your phone and never uploaded.
Self-hostable. Run the pipeline on your own infrastructure for full control.
